About Us

Project Artichoke was established in 2005, as Kerri Russell Garden Design. With a vision to integrate her designs with high quality construction, the business has evolved into Project Artichoke.

Based in Perth, Western Australia, we specialize primarily in residential design, but have the scope to look at anything from a simple planting layout to fully specified commercial designs.

Kerri focuses on delivering a boutique style service that encompasses value design, thorough detailing and individual attention.

From the consult, and right through the construction process, we will work with you and our trades to ensure we reach an outcome that surpasses all expectations.

With expertise and accredited qualifications in both Garden Design and Project Management, along with over 8 years experience as a Senior Landscape Designer for Tim Davies Landscaping, you can be sure that you are in professional hands.

Design Services

CONSULTATION

$300 +gst

The initial consultation is an opportunity to assess the existing garden and talk through your ideas for change. We can discuss practicalities and problems such as changes in level and alternative ways of dealing with them, as well as how you use the garden and the style we feel will suit you, your house and the site. A preliminary Sketch Idea of the garden design together with the Fee Proposal are sent to you. This shows the proposed garden design and also serves as a discussion document allowing you a further opportunity for input if you decide to proceed further.

CONCEPT PLAN

Starting from $1500 +gst

This is the second stage of the design process and is when we will turn the sketch idea, along with any changes you want to make, into a scaled cad drawing on the computer. We talk through any changes you may wish to make and clarify design details such as landscaping materials. This is a more accurate plan and will outline all the ideas involved, along with any elevations to help you visualise your new garden.

DETAIL PLANS

This is the third stage of the garden design process, incorporating any amendments agreed with you, showing all landscape details necessary for your contractor to be able to cost, set out and build the garden.
